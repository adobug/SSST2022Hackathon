What exactly is MedSite?

MedSite is an application that allows users to search and diagnose all visible or visual dermatological types of diseases or any changes on the body. It is very easy to use, so with just one photo taken ,the application reads which type of dermatological disease or other change is( details with a photo) and the user is automatically provided with an alternative: advice or instructions on self-treatment (if the case is harmless) or instructions for asking a doctor or specialist (if the case is not harmless). All that is needed is for the user to take a picture of a certain part of the body where the growth to some other change is located, and then he will get all the necessary information about it.

MedSite was created for the purpose of more efficient, faster and more accessible diagnosis of dermatological diseases in order for users to avoid the often complicated process of going to the doctor, and to get all the information they are interested in on a very simple way. The images used by MedSite are collected from real life, which doctors personally photographed on their patients and archived in our database. It is very important to emphasize that all data on various diseases and visible changes stored in MedSite are based on books of the medical faculty, not from the Internet. Therefore, this application is completely accurate and reliable.
